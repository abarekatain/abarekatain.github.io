Project Overview

The Deep Reinforcement Learning Course offered by Hugging Face covers both theoretical foundations and practical applications of Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL). The curriculum included hands-on training with prominent DRL libraries such as Stable Baselines3, RL Baselines3 Zoo, Sample Factory, and CleanRL. Engaged in training agents across diverse environments, including SnowballFight, Huggy the Doggo, VizDoom (Doom), and classic scenarios like Space Invaders and PyBullet. The course emphasized sharing trained agents on the Hugging Face Hub and participating in AI vs. AI challenges to evaluate agent performance.
