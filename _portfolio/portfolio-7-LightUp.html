“LightUp” is an independent(Indie) game project initiated by me and one of my friends, the Digital Brush Group. It is a memory game powered by Unity3D and targeted for android platforms. At each level of the game, the goal is to move the power source towards the power symbol, and light it up! All the objects in the level disappear as the game starts, so the challenge is to remember the location of everything beforehand.

LightUp project helped me develop my skills in almost every aspect. As a programmer, I enhanced my programming skills within a larger-scale development. As a team player, I could improve my soft skills such as time management, teamworking, and decision making, and learn how to deal with challenges, learn independently, and become self-driven. Finally, my biggest achievement in this project was the opportunity to think out of the box, be creative, and put this creativity into action.

“Light It Up” was nominated for “Game Design” and “Creativity” at the Independent Game Developers Festival(IGDF), Tehran, Iran for two consecutive years.
