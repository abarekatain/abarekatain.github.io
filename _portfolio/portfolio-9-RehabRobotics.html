Design and Development of a Passive Wrist and Forearm Rehabilitative Exoskeleton
" collection: portfolio order: 9 ---

My B.Sc. thesis project was to design and develop a passive wrist and forearm rehabilitative exoskeleton. As a group project, my team and I consulted with several experts in rehabilitation science to enhance our design, utilizing Autodesk Inventor and Catia. Also, we gained practical skills in manufacturing a device from scratch. As for measuring the movement of each joint of the device, I could write a program to use the sensor in Mouse instead of an encoder, as it is frictionless and often cheaper than encoders.
