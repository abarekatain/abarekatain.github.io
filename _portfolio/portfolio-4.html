This is one of the projects carried out during the course “Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)”. The aim of the project was to utilize CFD methods to perform transient heat transfer analysis for a semi-infinite hollow cylinder. For convenience, a quarter of the cylinder was considered based on symmetry.

The Finite-Volume method along with the Gauss-Seidel algorithm has been applied to solve the problem, and below is the temperature result for a set of boundary conditions animated in Tecplot.
