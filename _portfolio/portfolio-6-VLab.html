The aim of this project is to simulate the movement of a sphere inside a liquid under the force of gravity and the drag force caused by the fluid. In order to provide a virtual laboratory environment, two movable markers are located on the liquid tube and a chronometer is implemented. The user must measure the time required for the sphere to travel between the markers in order to calculate the terminal velocity.
