TABAN Application is the project assigned to me during my part-time job at Rayan Tahlil Company located at Isfahan Science and Technology Town, Iran. I was responsible for developing a Graphical User Interface(GUI) for visualizing the thermal parameters of the hot rolling process of Mobarakeh Steel Company. The application communicates with a background program which performs the calculations, and the results can be shown node-by-node, plotted, or exported.
